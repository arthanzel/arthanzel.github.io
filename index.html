I'm a programmer, a biologist, a mentor, and a well-rounded geek. I studied and lived at the University of Ottawa, where a horde of first-year residents used to come to me for math help. I've been coding since I was a kid, when I completed some curly braces on my father's unattended machine.

I studied biomedical science because I was fascinated by how living things resemble computers. Programming cells is a difficult, but satisfying problem to work on. I'm now looking for other difficult and satisfying problems. Perhaps your company has some. I can help solve them.
