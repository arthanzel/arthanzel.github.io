Martin Hanzel

I'm a programmer, a biologist, a mentor, and a well-rounded geek. I live at the University of Ottawa with a horde of first-year residents who come to me for math help. I've been coding since I was a kid, when I completed some curly braces on my dad's machine when he left his code unattended.

I am especially interested in how computer science relates to other disciplines. I spend my free time trying to quantify things like how DNA works, or why musical instruments sound the way they do, often making cool discoveries and forgetting about what I was doing earlier.
